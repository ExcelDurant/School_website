Why Choose Us?

Study on our Campus

Begin with a 12 months hands-on training in our well equipped IT labs at our campus in Bali, Douala. Learn about the core technologies, current industry standards and the productivity skills to become an efficient developer

Practical Internships

Apply your skills on projects at our partner companies locally and internationally in a 6 month paid internship program supervised by both Seven and the partner. You will benefit from the ability to apply your skills in the real business world as you express your talents.

Solve real challenges

Coming back for the last 6 months, you get to plan and execute the solution to an identified real world problem applying your skills both soft and hard to deliver a working solution. You'd be mentored and guided by one of our experts.
